◆ LoadAntennaPattern()

void LPT::Antenna::LoadAntennaPattern ( )

Set the Antenna Pattern object.

GPS SV(transmitter) antenna file definitions: The format for 1D (elevation) antenna patttern files is as follows: % angle [deg] gain [db] 0.0 gain1 . . . . angN gainN

The format for 2D antenna patttern files is as follows: (angles in deg, gain in dB)

-50 0.0 az2 ... azM 0.0 gain1,1 gain1,2 ... gain1,M . . . . elN gainN,1 gainN,2 ... gainN,M

2D antenna pattern -50 0.0 az2 ... azM 0.0 gain1,1 gain1,2 ... gain1,M . . . . elN gainN,1 gainN,2 ... gainN,M

1D antenna pattern 0.0 gain1 . . . . angN gainN
